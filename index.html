How to Play

build words, earn points, become the champion

01

Gather Players

2-4 players take turns building words on the 15x15 board.

02

Setup the Game

Enter player names, select number of players (2-4), and start the game.

03

The Board

The board has premium squares: DL (Double Letter), TL (Triple Letter), DW (Double Word), TW (Triple Word).

04

Place Tiles

Select a letter from your rack, then click a board cell. Choose Horizontal or Vertical direction.

05

Score Points

Each letter has points. Premium squares multiply your score. Submit your word to earn points!

06

Win the Game

The game ends when no tiles remain. The player with the highest score wins!
